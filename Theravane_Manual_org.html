Theravane Manual

Welcome to Theravane

Theravane is a secure feature rich smartphone-based App for healthcare professionals that is designed to optimize patient care and enhance collaboration among healthcare professionals.

This manual outlines the key features and functionalities of the Theravane App for Doctors.

Please note that you have to be a qualified Doctor to be granted sign up privileges to use the Theravane App.  Once you have been vetted by your Workspace, you will receive an email invitation to join the Theravane App as a user.  

We will send you an invite into your email which you will then click on a link and download Theravane on Android Play Store or IOS .

  • For new users: Complete the registration process via the "Sign Up" option.
  • For returning users: Click on the "Login" button.

Logging into Theravane App

After signing up you can now launch the Theravane App for better patient care on your smartphone device.

                     

Enter your username and password and click login.

- Passwords must include at least one character from each of the following categories:

  • Uppercase letters (A-Z)
  • Lowercase letters (a-z)
  • Numbers (0-9)
  • Special characters (e.g., !@#$%^&*()_+{}[]|:;<>,.?~)
  • example, Admin@123

Screen Overviews

Landing Page

On opening the Theravane App, you will be presented with the landing page as seen below.  Theravane will display your name prominently at the top of the page.  There are four tabs at the bottom of the page namely, Message, Episode, Dashboard and Profile.  

                

TABS EXPLAINED

Messages

The "Message" tab will display all the conversations that you are involved in. These may be Private conversations, Group Chats and Episode of Care Chats. The Chat system built into Theravane is very similar to WhatsApp, allowing seamless interactions with other healthcare professionals. By tapping on one of these you will be taken into that chat. The chat will allow for text conversations and also allow for attachments that can be sourced from your photo library, PDF documents, camera photos and short video clips.

Episodes

This tab will display all the Episodes of Care that you have access to and are currently working on. Tapping on one of these Episodes of Care will take you into the Episode of Care.

Dashboard

The dashboard in the Theravane app provides a comprehensive overview of Orders sent to Lancet Pathology and Invictus Radiology as linked diagnostic healthcare providers on the app in status of New Orders that are displayed, In Progress orders that are received and looked at also Awaiting Results status for soon to be completed Orders that are listed in the chronological order.

Profile Tab shows the name of the user on top with a filter and search option and this is a section dedicated to displaying and managing user-specific information tab which acts as a centralized place where users can invite other colleagues to have access to their patients and also create a new group or add members .

After you opened up an Episode of Care you will see on top a banner of options to choose from as follows:

Episode

An episode is used to track all clinical interactions, treatments, and services provided within a specific time frame, including a pool of ICD-10 codes and CPT-4 procedures.

               

Clinical Notes

Clinical notes are written records created by healthcare providers to document patient interactions, assessments, treatments, and progress over time.

 

Vitals

Vital signs, often referred to as "vitals," are clinical measurements that provide essential information about a patient’s physiological status. They are key indicators of a patient's health and are routinely assessed in clinical settings

                     

Orders

orders refer to instructions that healthcare professionals, such as doctors, nurses, or pharmacists, create for a patient's treatment

Orders options are Pathology, Radiology and Others :

                         

                                                   

Forms

A list of Forms that are attached to the Episode of Care. Possible forms are the Triage Form. The Triage form is a tool used in healthcare settings, particularly in emergency departments, to prioritize patient care based on the severity of their condition. The goal of triage is to assess patients quickly and efficiently, determining who needs immediate attention and who can safely wait for care.

                       

Patient

Patient Information is the comprehensive set of data that records an individual's demographics, and other relevant details such as Next of Kin etc.

             

         

Chat Media

Any Media that is shared in chat attached to the Episode of Care is visible here for quick access.

                                                 

Admin Notes

Administrative notes are for the recording of administrative information about the Episode of Care. This can be the recording of a Patient's movement within a Hospital, or the recording information about an Authorisation code obtained from a Medical Funder.

                     

Members

A section where administrators or users can view, manage, and interact with the list of individuals who are part of the Episode of Care. It provides essential information about the members and often includes management features such as adding, editing, or removing members.         

               

                         

Previous Episodes

a Previous Episodes section refers to a digital archive where clinicians can access and review a patient’s past medical encounters, treatments, consultations, or procedures.

                         

How To:

Create a new Order once in the Episode of Care (see section How to get into an EOC) Use the following steps to create an Order.

  1. Navigate to the Episode of Care:
  • Navigate to the Episode of Care for the Patient, by either selecting the Episode of Care from the Episodes list or searching for the Episode of Care.
  •        

         

  1. Access the Episode of Care:
  • In the Episode of Care, look for the section labeled “Orders” .
  • Tapping on the FAB blue menu dot will bring up a list of Menu options

  1. Select the "Create Order" Option:
  • Click the create order menu option to initiate a new order entry form.
  1. Enter Order Details:
  • Tap on the “Order Type” dropdown and select amongst Pathology, Other and Radiology
  • For Pathology and Radiology orders, tap under Pathology or Radiology test required. This will bring up a new screen allowing for the selection of the tests that are to be ordered.
  • Once all the Tests have been selected tap the “Add Selected Items”. This will return you to the order screen and display the Tests that were selected.
  • For Radiology Orders, the Motivation for the Tests needs to be included.
  • Select when you want the order to be completed.

  1. Confirmation:
  • The system will display a confirmation message or notification on healthcare providers once the order is successfully created.
  • The new order will now be visible and displayed under the patient’s Episode of Care in the record

  1. Track Order Status:
  • Users can monitor the status of the order (In progress, completed, etc.) through the dashboard

Adding a new Clinical Note to an Episode of Care

On the very same banner look for a Clinical Notes tab :

  • Select the clinical note tab on the banner. or once inside the selected episode on the blue circle menu, look for an option labeled “Add Clinical Note”
  • Click this option to open the clinical note entry form .
  • Enter the clinical notes and click on add notes

Adding new Members to an Episode of Care

Access the Episode of Care:

  • Navigate to the "Episodes" section within the patient’s record.
  • Select the specific episode to which you want to add new members

Locate the " Members" Option:

  • Within the selected episode click the blue circle menu look for an option labeled "Add Members" then choose the member by checking on a box .
  • Click on “Add Users” to proceed.

                    

Updating an Episode of Care

Search for the Patient:

  • Use the search menu to find the patient’s record by entering their name
  • Click on the patient’s profile to open their medical record.

Locate the Episode :

  • Navigate to the "Episodes" section within the patient’s record.
  • Identify the specific episode that needs updating and click on it to view the details.
  • Click on a blue circle button and tap on Edit Episode
  • Click this option to open the editable fields
  • Make Necessary Updates

The same process will be primarily used for adding members, updating episodes, updating patient information and all the options on the above picture.

Starting a Chat with a fellow colleague

To chat directly with another Workspace user

  • On the Landing Screen Tap the Fab (this is the blue icon with three dashed lines located on the bottom right-hand corner of the page).  
  • Tapping on this icon will bring up three options 1. search icon, 2. inverted pyramid icon and 3. + icon.
  • Please tap on the + icon.

     

  • Tapping on this icon will bring up a menu with four options, 1. Create new Episode of Care, 2.Add new Patient, 3. Chat with another Workspace User, 4. Create a New Group.  Please choose the “Chat with another Workspace User” option.  
  • Tapping on the third option “Chat with another Workspace User” to call up a directory list of registered users you can contact directly.  
  • Please tap the name of the User you want to message. This will bring up a new page with a prompt to chat with the specific user.

  • Tap the “Chat with User” button.  

             

  • Tapping the Button will bring up a page with the name of the user you are messaging displayed on top and an option to add an attachment at the bottom left corner {paperclip icon) or free text that is entered in the “write a message” space.
  • You also have the option to attach Files, Videos & Photos exactly like Whatsapp.
